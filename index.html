How I Will Rate the Games

I will rate the games for each mechanic from 1-10. 10 being done well and 1 being done poorly along with this I will also give my reasoning as to why I gave it that score. Each category will be rated in the order as listed here.

Gameplay: How good is the gameplay.

Music: How good does the music fit the game and how good the music is.

Mechanics: Are the mechanics good and are they thrown in for the sake of a mechanic.

Graphics: How good the graphics are for the type of graphics they are. Etc pixel art.

User interface: Are the menus good to use and how cluttered is the screen during gameplay.

Story/Characters: How well done the story and How well done the Characters are developed throughout the story

Bugs: How many bugs/How many game breaking bugs. Etc deleting save files, Getting access to places or things to early (if can be easily found by normal play and not for the sake of a speedrun)
